## How to Update Content

### Blog Posts

Creating new blog posts is easy with MDX (Markdown + JSX):

<QuestionAnswer
  question="How do I create a new blog post?"
  answer="Create a new .mdx file in the /content/blog/ directory. Add frontmatter with title, excerpt, date, and other metadata. Then write your content using Markdown. Deploy your changes and the post appears automatically!"
/>

<QuestionAnswer
  question="What's MDX and why use it?"
  answer="MDX combines Markdown's simplicity with React components. You can use standard formatting plus custom components like callout boxes, image layouts, and Q&A sections. It's powerful yet easy to learn."
/>

Check out the [complete feature showcase](/blog/example-blog-post-with-all-features) to see all available components.

## Analytics & Tracking

Understand your website traffic with Google Analytics 4:

### What's Being Tracked

**Conversions** (High Priority):
- Phone calls clicked
- Forms submitted
- Newsletter signups
- Downloads

**Engagement** (Medium Priority):
- Page views
- Time on site
- Scroll depth
- Video plays

**Errors** (For Optimization):
- Form errors
- Exit points
- Bounce rate

### Viewing Your Data

1. Go to [analytics.google.com](https://analytics.google.com)
2. Select your property
3. View real-time and historical data
4. Create custom reports

<PracticalTip>
**Analytics Best Practice**

Check your analytics weekly for the first month, then monthly after that. Look for trends in traffic sources, popular pages, and conversion rates. Use these insights to refine your content strategy.
</PracticalTip>
